Bhavgeet

An AI tool that generated Music Playlist based on Human emotions

The objective of this project is to generate a music playlist based on the emotions of the human facial expression. Facial expressions are the key to analyze the current psychology of the person. In this composition, we discuss a user-intuitive, smart music player. This player captures the facial expressions of a person working on the computer and identifies the current emotion. Intuitively the music is played for the user to relax them. The music player holds into account the foreground processes which the person is executing on the computer

  • Date: February 2021
  • Intership:SDG Internship
  • Category: Research oriented
